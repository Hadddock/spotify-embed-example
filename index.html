SPOTIFY EMBED EXAMPLES

Users who have not logged into Spotify in their browser (open this page in incognito mode if you're already logged in) will see an overlaid static advertisement for spotify each time they pause a song. They will also only be able to listen to 30 second samples of each song. If users are automatically logged in in their browser, they can listen to the full track and see no advertisements when pressing the pause / play button.
